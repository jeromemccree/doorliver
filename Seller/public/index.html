Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do Drivers pick up the item?

    For Local Businesses, the Item will already be paid for through our platform. When the driver arrives he will have a verification code. You will Recieve payout at the end of the day.

    For Enterprises, we have cards that our drivers pay with through your POS System.

  • How do we handle returns?

    We'll notify you if someone wants to return something. If you approve then the customer will bring it to your store and we'll issue a refund once you receive the item. We plan to return items using our drivers in the future.
