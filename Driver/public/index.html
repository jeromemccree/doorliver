Common Questions

What is Doorliver?
Doorliver is an on-demand delivery eCommerce service. We enable people to purchase anything within a 20-mile radius and be delivered in less than 3 hours.

Waiting for a package that you're excited about receiving can be a pain. Sometimes your package will arrive within 2-5 days of purchasing it, now instead of waiting 2 days you only wait 3 hours. We can sell any products from any brick and mortar stores like Walmart, Macy's, and Home Depot to local book stores and jewelry shops.

Why Partner with Doorliver?
Partnering with Doorliver is a great opportunity for anyone looking for a flexible schedule. Previous delivery experience is not required, but we encourage drivers from other delivery or ridesharing services such as Uber, Doordash etc to Apply. Let's get you on the road. Apply today! *Hourly minimum if requirements met.
How Does it Work?
Login to the Doorliver website. We send you a few orders at retailers location. Pay with Doorlivery card that we issue you. Deliver to happy customers! Easy as that!
What do I need to get started?
Doorliver Partners will be independent contractors and must have a valid driver's license, minimum auto insurance and atleast 18 years old or older.

Doorliver Partners will also use their own reliable car, iPhone or Android phone and their data and text plan. Previous delivery experience is not required, but we encourage drivers from other delivery or ridesharing services such as Sprig, Caviar, Munchery to Apply
